About Us

Who we are

Welcome to Alphadiella

Alphadiella Limited is an indigenous Engineering, procurement and Construction Company with 100% local content capacity to offer professional services to Oil, Gas, Construction and Process industries in Nigeria and beyond. Our areas of expertise include Process Plant designs and simulations, Process Safety & Loss prevention, Facility integrity maintenance and management, Technical Manpower supply and staffing, Procurement, Engineering consultancy, Installations and Construction.

Our Mission

To deliver quality Engineering, Construction and Procurement services in a safe manner to meet our client’s need.

Our Vision

To be a leading Engineering, Procurement and Construction Company in Nigeria through quality services delivery in a safest manner to our clients in line with international standards practices.
